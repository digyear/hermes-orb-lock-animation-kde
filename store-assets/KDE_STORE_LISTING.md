## Summary

A compact animated Hermes orb illustration for KDE Plasma 6 desktop and lock screens.

## Description

Hermes Orb Animation adapts the rotating orb illustration from the official Hermes Agent website as a compact KDE Plasma 6 animated wallpaper.

Features:

- compact 338-pixel maximum artwork height;
- centered blue-and-white illustration on a black background;
- seamless 2.8-second loop;
- software-decoded animated image playback to avoid VP9 hardware-decoder artifacts;
- works in both the Plasma desktop and lock-screen wallpaper selectors;
- fully self-contained after installation.

Desktop and lock-screen wallpaper selections are configured independently in Plasma System Settings.

This is an unofficial community adaptation and is not an official Nous Research or KDE product.
